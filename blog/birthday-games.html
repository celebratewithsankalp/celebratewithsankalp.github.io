    Comprehensive Guide To Birthday Games

    Posted by Nishant Nath at 3 Mar, 2019 Last Updated: 3 Mar, 2019

    Birthday Games are as old as the concept of birthdays itself. Every celebration calls for an activity or involvement event which gets the participants/guests involved. Our comprehensive guide to most popular games will not only help you decide which games you want to include in your birthday party it will also give you a detailed guide on the rules of the game. So no cheating ... not even by the birthday boy!

    • Pin The Tail

      • Pin The Tail is also known by names like Pin The Tail Of The Donkey or Pin The Tail On Eeyore is a popular game that involves putting up a large picture of a donkey or elephant or any animal for that matter and having a blindfolded child attempt to pin the paper tail at the correct location. The blindfolded child is often helped by the other children in the room by giving hints using only 4 words: "up, down, left, right". This game can also be played in teams.     • Musical Chairs

      • Musical Chairs is a game of elimination that entices competitive spirit in participants and involves a good deal of physical activity which makes kids very happy. The rules are very simple. A group of chairs are arranged back-to-back and an adult is designated as music co-ordinator. While the music is playing the kids need to dance and keep moving around the chairs with atleast 2 feet of distance between chair and themselves and when the music stops everyone needs to occupy a seat. In each turn a chair is removed such that number of chairs is always 1 less than the number of kids in play. During each round, 1 child is unfortunately removed from the competition and finally last person remaining on the chair is the winner.
